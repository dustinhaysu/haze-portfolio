Why do I need a website?

As the old adage goes it's not what you know but who you know

Regardless of what you do or want to do for a living developing a professional network is a big step forward towards establishing a financially abundant life.

There is a lot of competition out there when it comes to finding good work with good companies. Every time you head out your front door to meet the day you are presented with opportunities to improve your situation. In some careers, a portfolio website is as necessary as a resumè and professional references. In other fields, it makes you the one person out of a thousand that gets noticed. It’s your best foot forward. It’s a website that can be printed onto a business card or added to the top of a resumè. It’s something extra that sends a potential employer clicking around your meticulously curated professional universe instead of sifting through more applications. It's the business card that gets a magnet on the fridge or a pin on the bulletin board. It’s what puts you on a shortlist and keeps your name on top of the stack.
